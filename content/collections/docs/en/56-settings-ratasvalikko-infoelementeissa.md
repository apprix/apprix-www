<p>The collapsible <strong>Settings</strong> toolbar located in the top-left corner of the element editing view is available in info elements, such as</p><ul><li><p>Intro</p></li><li><p>Info Full Background</p></li><li><p>Info Half Background</p></li></ul><p>The toolbar allows you to adjust the position of text boxes (up, down, or centre), colour, or background. Please note that the Settings toolbar is only visible in the element editing view.</p><p><strong>Tip:</strong> You can also choose to display both text boxes at once in the learner view of the module, instead of the default of one text box at a time.</p><ol start="1"><li><p>Click the gear symbol and the toolbar opens. Adjust settings by hovering the cursor over the toolbar functions and clicking the desired option from the dropdown menus that appear.</p></li><li><p>Close the toolbar by clicking the X on the left side of the view.</p></li></ol><h3>How does the Settings toolbar work in info elements?</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2024/05/5.6-Settings-ratasvalikko-infoelementeissa.gif"></p>
