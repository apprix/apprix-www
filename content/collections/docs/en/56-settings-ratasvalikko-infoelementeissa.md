title: '5.6 Settings Bar in Info Elements'
---
In <b>Edit Element Contents</b> <b>view,</b> you can find the <b>Settings bar</b> on the top left corner in several info elements, such as
<ul>
 <li>Intro, </li>
 <li>Info Full Background and </li>
 <li>Info Half Background</li>
</ul>
The purpose of this tool is to allow you to adjust the position (top, middle, bottom), color and background color of the text boxes. Note that the Settings bar is only visible in Edit Element Contents mode.

<b>Tip!</b> You can also choose to show both boxes at once.
<ol>
 <li>Click the tool button. The Settings bar will appear on the window.</li>
 <li>Adjust the settings by hovering the mouse on top of the bar and when ready, close the bar by clicking the X on the left side.</li>
</ol>
