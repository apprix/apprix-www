title: '7.24.2 How do I add a video from YouTube?'
---
<ol>
 <li>Open the video, click the <strong>Share</strong> button below it and select <strong>Embed</strong>.</li>
 <li>You will now see the embed code. If you want to prevent the user from skipping through the video, scroll down a little and uncheck "Show player controls".</li>
 <li>Copy the embed code and paste it into the <strong>Paste video embed code/iframe</strong> tab field in the video element's <strong>Change Video/Audio</strong> field.</li>
</ol>
