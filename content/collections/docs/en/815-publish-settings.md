<strong>What is the Publish Settings feature?</strong>

Publish Settings is a tool that allows you to define and save important metadata for your training modules. This data is used in module management, reporting, and integrations with other systems. The data to be collected is defined on a customer-specific basis during the tool’s implementation.

<strong>With this feature, you can:</strong>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Enrich data:</strong> Collect course-related information that isn’t directly tied to learning content but is important for administration.</li>
 <li><strong>Automate data transfer:</strong> Automatically send course completion data to external systems, such as HR or LMS platforms.</li>
 <li><strong>Improve reporting:</strong> Generate more detailed and versatile reports when additional information is linked to courses.</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Instructions for using the feature</strong></h4>
When publishing a training module, you first select the training type. Each type has its own customised form with fields relevant to that specific type.
For example, “<strong>Internal Training</strong>” may show different fields than “<strong>External Training</strong>.” By filling in these fields, you ensure all necessary data is saved with the course.
<ol>
 <li><strong> Open Publish Settings:</strong>
When you publish a module by clicking the Publish button, the Publish Settings dialogue opens automatically. You can also fill in the information in advance by opening the dialogue via the gear icon in the module’s Edit mode.</li>
 <li><strong>Select training type:</strong>
Choose the most appropriate training type for the situation. For example, “Internal Training” may show different fields than “External Training.” Filling in these fields ensures all required data is saved with the course.</li>
 <li><strong>Fill in the fields:</strong>
Fill in the mandatory (*) and relevant optional fields on the form.</li>
 <li><strong>Save:</strong>
Save the settings and publish the module. The data is now stored with the module and used according to the defined specifications.</li>
</ol>
<strong> </strong><strong>Creating custom training types and fields</strong>

The system is flexible, and your organisation can create training types and data fields tailored to your specific needs. If you feel the current options need new functionalities, please contact us!
