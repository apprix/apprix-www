<p><strong>What is the Publish Settings feature?</strong></p>

<p>Publish Settings is a tool that allows you to define and save important metadata for your training modules. This information is used for module management, reporting, and integrations with other systems. The data collected is defined on a per-customer basis when the tool is deployed.</p>

<p>With this feature, you can:</p>
<ul>
 <li><strong>Enrich data:</strong> Collect information about a course that is not directly related to the learning content, but is important for management purposes.</li>
 <li><strong>Automate data transfer:</strong> Automatically transmit course completion data to external systems, such as human resources (HR) or learning management (LMS) systems.</li>
 <li><strong>Improve reporting:</strong> Get more detailed and versatile reports when additional information has been associated with courses.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Instructions for using the feature</h3>
<p>When you are publishing a training module, you first select the <strong>training type (Training type)</strong>. Each training type has its own customised form with the relevant data fields for that particular type.</p>

<p>For example, "Internal training" may show different fields than "External training". By filling in these fields, you ensure that all the necessary information is saved with the course.</p>
<ol>
 <li><strong>Open Publish Settings:</strong> When you publish a module by pressing the Publish button, the Publish Settings dialog opens automatically. You can also fill in the information in advance by opening the Publish Settings dialog in the module edit mode via the gear menu.</li>
 <li><strong>Select training type:</strong> Select the most appropriate training type for the situation. For example, "Internal training" may show different fields than "External training".</li>
 <li><strong>Fill in the fields:</strong> Fill in the mandatory (*) and any optional fields that appear in the form.</li>
 <li><strong>Save:</strong> Save the settings and publish the module. The information is now saved with the module and will be used as configured.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Creating your own training types and fields</strong></p>

<p>The system is flexible, and training types and data fields tailored to your organisation's specific needs can be created. If you feel that the current options are not sufficient or you need new functionality, please get in touch with us!</p>
