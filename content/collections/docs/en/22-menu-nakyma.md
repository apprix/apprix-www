<p>After logging in, you will arrive at <b>Menu View,</b> where all course modules in your organisation's Builder account have been listed. In this view, you may:</p>

<ul>
 <li>create a new module from<strong> <a href="#creatingmodules">Create</a> </strong>located in the sidebar on the left</li>
 <li>copy an existing module from <strong><a href="#duplicatingmodules">Duplicate</a> </strong>located in the sidebar on the right</li>
 <li>search for modules with the <strong>Search bar</strong> or with <a href="#edittags"><strong>Tags</strong></a>
<ul>
 <li>With tags, you can bookmark modules or module groups so that they are easier to find using the tag as a search criteria.</li>
 <li>For example, a language, a department, a branch or a course subject can be used as a tag.</li>
</ul>
</li>
 <li>browse, edit and view existing modules <b>you have editing or viewing rights to</b>
<ul>
 <li>Next to published modules, you can find the <strong><a href="#publishingmodules">Published button</a></strong></li>
 <li><span>From the<strong> <a href="#previewingmodules">Preview button</a> </strong></span>next to modules, you will be able to preview a module</li>
 <li>If you hold module edit or statistics viewing rights to specific modules, you may also find <strong><a href="#stats">Stats buttons</a></strong> next to published modules where you can view module statistics.</li>

If you are an admin user in your organization, you may also find the <strong><a href="#administrate">Administrate</a> button </strong>located in the sidebar on the left, from where you can edit user management settings.
<h4><strong>How to use Quick Filters tool to find your modules?</strong></h4>
The <strong>Quick Filters</strong> tool helps you organise and filter modules and online trainings in Apprix Builder. It makes it easier to find the content you need quickly and efficiently.

<strong> </strong>

<strong>What can you do with it?</strong>

With Quick Filters, you can:
<ul>
 <li><strong>sort modules</strong> alphabetically or by date</li>
 <li><strong>filter your own modules</strong>, i.e. the ones you’ve created</li>
 <li><strong>filter editable modules</strong>, where you have editing rights</li>
 <li><strong>filter modules with unresolved comments</strong></li>
 <li><strong>filter by publication status</strong>: all, published, unpublished</li>
</ul>
<strong>Where to find the feature?</strong>

You’ll find the Quick Filters tool in the <strong>top right corner</strong> of the <em>Menu</em> &gt;  <em>All Modules</em> window in Builder. Click the icon to open the filter menu. The selected filter(s) will appear in the filter text field at the top left of the <em>Menu</em> &gt; <em>All Modules</em> window.

<strong>Tip:</strong> To narrow your search, try combining multiple filters — for example, your unpublished modules with unresolved comments.
