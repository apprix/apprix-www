<p>At first glance, the <strong>Slider Write Text</strong> element resembles the <a href="/en/help/721-feedback">Feedback element</a> in terms of functionality, but differs from it in terms of its usage features. The element can be used, for example, to measure learners' understanding of the subject areas covered in a module. In this case, the learner evaluates their own understanding of the topics covered in the module on a scale of one to five. The main difference from the Feedback element is that if the learner sets the slider to one or two, they can describe in their own words which topic they need more information about.</p><p>The element can also be used, for example, for data collection by using the same question and topics after a certain period of time, in order to find out how well the learners feel a strategy topic from Q1 has been implemented compared to the topic from Q3.</p><h3>Element in preview mode</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/08/Slider_Write_Text_FI-1.gif"></p><h3>Element settings in edit view:</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/08/Slider_Write_Text_FI_Edit-1.gif"></p>
