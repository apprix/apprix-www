<p>The <strong>Rate Comments</strong> element is placed after the Comments element. It displays the feedback and opinions that learners have added to the Comments element in the module.</p><p>Learners can evaluate and review comments and averages by clicking the dropdown in the bar at the top of the page. By clicking the topic they want to rate, a popup with five stars opens. Here they can rate the comment.</p><p>Learners can change the order of comments by clicking the right side of the bar.</p><p>By clicking:</p><ul><li><p>Star - comments are sorted from highest to lowest rated. (Default)</p></li><li><p>Calendar - comments are sorted from newest to oldest.</p></li></ul><p><strong>Note!</strong></p><p>In the dropdown menu, topics are grouped by Comments elements. It is therefore recommended to name the comment elements so that it is clear from the topics shown in the dropdown which Comments element they belong to.</p><p></p><h3>How can a learner view and rate comments?</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2025/03/Comments-rate.gif"></p><h3>Remember to name the Comments elements!</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2025/03/Rate-Comments1.png"></p>
