title: '3.2 Naming a Module'
---
<p>Name your module on the top left by clicking the text. Press <b>Save</b> when done.</p>
It is always a good idea to name your module first when starting to edit a new module. This way, you can easily find it in the Menu view if you need to interrupt your session and continue later.
