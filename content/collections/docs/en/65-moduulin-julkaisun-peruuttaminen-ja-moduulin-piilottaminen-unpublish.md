title: '6.5 Cancelling a Module Publication and Hiding a Module (Unpublish)'
---
<p>If you want to remove a module from publication but keep it in Builder, this is done by clicking the <strong>Published</strong> button. When you hover the cursor over the button, the text changes to <strong>Unpublish</strong>. When you click the button, Builder confirms whether you want to take the module off publication. After this, the module can no longer be accessed via its link unless you publish the module again.</p><h3>How do you take a module off publication (Unpublish)?</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2024/05/Unpublish-module-1.gif"></p>
