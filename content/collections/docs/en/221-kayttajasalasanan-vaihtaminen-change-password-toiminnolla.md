<p class="p1">You can change your password at any time by selecting from <b>Menu</b> view the “<b>Change Password</b>” option.</p>
<p class="p3"><b>Change Password</b> pop-up window will be opened. Fill in the requested credentials (old and new passwords) and press <b>Continue</b>. You will be informed that the password has been changed, and the <b>Menu</b> view will be opened.</p>
<p class="p5"><b>Note!</b> The new password should contain at least 8 characters, upper- and lower-case letters, number(s) and special characters.</p>
