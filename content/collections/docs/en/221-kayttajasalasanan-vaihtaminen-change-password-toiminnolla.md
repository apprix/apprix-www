title: '2.2.1 Changing Your Password with the Change Password Function'
---
<p>You can change your account password at any time by clicking <strong>Change Password</strong> in the <strong>Menu</strong> view.</p><p>The <strong>Change Password</strong> popup window opens. Fill in the required information (new and old password) and click <strong>Continue</strong>. You will receive a notification that the password has been changed and the <strong>Menu</strong> view will open.</p><p><strong>Note!</strong> The new password must contain at least 8 characters, uppercase and lowercase letters, at least one number, and a special character.</p><h3>How do you change your Builder password?</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2025/03/2.2.1-Change-Password-Function-1.gif"></p>
