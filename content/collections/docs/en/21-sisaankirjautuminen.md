<p>The layout of Builder’s login page has been updated. The login option <strong>Single Sign-On</strong> has been moved from the bottom of the page to below the page title, where it is now available alongside the <strong>Password </strong>option.</p><h3>Login - Builder Login (Password)</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/login1.png"></p><p>Log in with your personal credentials at: <a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://builder.apprix.fi/b/"><strong>https://builder.apprix.fi/b/</strong></a></p><p>The login window will open.</p><p>If you select the ‘<strong>Password’</strong> option, enter your username and password and then press the <strong>Continue</strong> button. After this, Builder’s <strong>All Modules</strong> window will open.</p><p><strong>Note!</strong> Keep your password safe, as you currently cannot access Builder if the password is lost. If this happens, contact <strong>helpdesk@apprix.fi</strong> and request a new password. Alternatively, you can request a new password from someone in your company who has user‑management rights. When you receive the new password by email, you will be asked to change it.</p><p>If you select the ‘<strong>Single Sign-On’</strong> option, enter your email address and press the <strong>Continue</strong> button. The Single Sign-On window will open. Log in to the service. After that, the Builder <strong>All Modules</strong>  window will open.</p><p></p><h3>Login - Builder Login (Single Sign-On)</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::login-sso.png"></p><p></p>
