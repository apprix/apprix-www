title: '7.39 Order Expand'
---
<strong>Order Expand</strong> element is similar to the <strong>Order</strong>-element, with the addition of a couple of new features. In this element you can use up to 8 items (in the Order element max 6 items) and in the text boxes you can add a long text that grows or shrinks when you click on it.
