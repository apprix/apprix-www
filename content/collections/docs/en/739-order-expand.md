<p>The <strong>Order Expand</strong> element is similar to the <strong>Order</strong> element, with a couple of new features added. In this element you can use up to 8 items (the Order element has a max of 6 items) and the text boxes can contain long text that expands or collapses when clicked.</p><h3>Element in published mode</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2022/04/Order-Expand.gif"></p>
