title: '7.24.1 How do I add a video from the Vimeo video service?'
---
<p>We recommend using embed codes from Vimeo. Apprix has a business account through which we can upload videos so that they can only be viewed from within Builder course modules and the videos are not visible elsewhere. If your organization does not have a Vimeo account and you do not want to embed a video from, for example, YouTube, follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
 <li>Contact: <strong>helpdesk@apprix.fi</strong> and send the videos to Apprix (preferably in MP4 format)</li>
 <li>We will add the videos as hidden on Apprix's Vimeo account and send you the embed codes for adding the videos to your module. We can also add the videos directly to the correct locations if needed.</li>
</ol>

<p>If you want to know how to embed a video from your own Vimeo channel into Builder, see the FAQ:</p>

<p><a href="/faq">/faq</a>: "<strong>How do I embed a video from my own Vimeo channel?</strong>"</p>
