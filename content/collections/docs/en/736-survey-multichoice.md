<p><strong>Survey Multichoice</strong> is a survey element. The element allows you to present a question that the learner must answer by selecting one or more of the available options. Once the options have been selected, the learner clicks the forward button on the right side of the element, and an info box for a final summary or additional information appears. The box can be used to thank the learner for providing feedback and/or to give additional information about the topics covered in the element.</p><h3>Element editing instructions</h3><ul><li><p>Write the survey title and question/scenario in the text field on the left side of the window.</p></li><li><p>Add a background image by clicking the <strong>Change image</strong> icon in the upper right corner of the element's image field.</p></li><li><p><p>Click the <strong>Element settings</strong> option from the orange gear menu in the upper part of the element's edit view.</p><ul><li><p>Add, remove, edit, or reorder answer options in the <strong>Element settings</strong> window. New checkboxes will appear on the right side of the element.</p></li></ul></p></li><li><p>Click the gear icon in the upper left corner of the element. The <strong>Option settings</strong> window opens. In the window, you can set the requirement for selecting one or alternatively multiple answer options before the learner can proceed.</p></li><li><p>Click the question mark icon in the upper right corner of the element. The text box/final summary opens. In the text field you can thank the learner for providing feedback and/or give additional information about the task. You can also leave the box empty. In that case, the text box will not be shown to the learner at the end of the task.</p></li></ul><p></p><h3>Element in published mode</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/08/Survey_Multichoice_FI-1.gif"></p><h3>Element settings in edit view:</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/08/Survey_Multichoice_Edit_FI-1.gif"></p><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/06/survey1.png"></p><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/06/Survey2.png"></p><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/06/Survey3.png"></p>
