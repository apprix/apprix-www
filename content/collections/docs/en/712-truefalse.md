<p>In the <strong>True/False</strong> element, a statement or question is presented, which can be set as correct or incorrect from the orange <strong>gear icon</strong> in the edit view.</p><p>Examples of use cases:</p><ul><li><p>True or false? / Correct or incorrect? / Yes or no? – A statement to which the learner responds by thumbing correct or incorrect.</p></li><li><p>A question to which the learner responds by thumbing correct or incorrect.</p></li></ul><p><strong>Tip:</strong> The element is easy to use, as you do not need to think up incorrect statements in addition to correct ones. For example, you can divide a long topic or paragraph so that the first part becomes the statement and the latter part becomes the answer.</p><h3>Element in preview mode.</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/08/TrueFalse_FI-1.gif"></p><h3>Element settings in edit mode.</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/08/TrueFalse_Edit_FI-1.gif"></p>
