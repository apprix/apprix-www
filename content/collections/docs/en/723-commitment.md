<p>The <strong>Commitment</strong> element consists of text and a checkbox that the learner must tick before proceeding. The element can be used, for example, when you want to ask the learner to commit to certain guidelines or rules, and to encourage them to ask for help if anything remains unclear. Like all other performance data, the learner's commitment is saved to the module's statistics.</p><h3>Element editing instructions</h3><p>Add a background image and edit the commitment text.</p><p></p><h3>Element in preview mode</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2021/08/Sitoumus_small.gif"></p>
