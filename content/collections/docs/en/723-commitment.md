In the <b>Commitment</b> element, the trainee must give his/her commitment by ticking the box to proceed and finish the training.

The element can be used for example to ask the trainee to commit to following instructions or guidelines presented in the training and to commit to asking help from a superior if anything is unclear. Just like everything else, commitment data is saved to the course statistics.

Add the background image and edit the commitment text.
