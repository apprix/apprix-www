<p>These are <strong>elements</strong> – the building blocks that make up your Builder course. There are many different types of editable elements, and in practice they appear as pages to course learners.<br><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2020/05/elements_group-1-400x168.jpg"></p><p></p><p>Elements form modules. 1 course = 1 module</p><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2020/02/moduuli_rakenne-1-600x48.jpg"></p><p></p><p>Long training modules can be structured into more easily navigable <strong>submodule</strong> structures. In this case, the module structure is divided into a so-called main module and the submodules branching from it, which in practice represent sections or topic areas of the course.</p><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2020/02/esimerkkikoulutus_moduulinakyma_1-1-600x273.jpg"></p><p></p>
