<p>The image library (<strong>Image Library</strong>) has a folder structure. You can add, rename, and delete folders by clicking the gear icon in the image library.</p><p>In the image library you can:</p><ul><li><p>add and remove images</p></li><li><p>add, rename, and delete folders by clicking the gear icon in the image library</p></li><li><p>add or remove tags from images</p></li><li><p>add images to a subfolder and move them from one subfolder to another</p></li><li><p>search for images by their names or tags (works across folders and shows which folder the image is in)</p></li><li><p>choose to display 50/100/all images</p></li><li><p>sort and filter images when searching (filters are retained when searching and when switching folders)</p></li><li><p>hide image names</p></li><li><p>upload images of any size</p></li></ul><h3>How does the Image Library work?</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2024/06/elementin-taustakuva-1.gif"></p>
