title: '5.3.3 How does the Image Library work?'
---
The <strong>Image Library</strong> has a folder structure. You can add, rename and delete folders by clicking on the Image Library gear icon.

In the <strong>Image Library</strong>, you can:
<ul>
 <li>add and remove images</li>
 <li>add, rename, and delete folders by clicking on the Image Library's gear icon</li>
 <li>add or remove tags from the images</li>
 <li>add an image into a subfolder and drag it from one subfolder to another</li>
 <li>search for the images by their names or tags (works across folders and shows which folder the image is in)</li>
 <li>choose whether to show 50/100/all images</li>
 <li>sort and filter when searching for the images (filters are preserved when searching and changing folders)</li>
 <li>hide image names</li>
 <li>upload images of any size</li>
</ul>
