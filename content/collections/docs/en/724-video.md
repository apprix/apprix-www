<p>You can embed a video directly into the <strong>Video</strong> element by copying the embed code from a streaming service (e.g. YouTube or Vimeo). Open the element and click the <strong>Change Video/Audio</strong> field. Select the left tab <strong>Paste video embed code/iframe</strong> and paste the video embed code from YouTube or Vimeo into it. Press Done and the video will appear on the page.</p>

<p><strong>NOTE! Builder does not support embedding YouTube playlists, only individual videos.</strong></p>

<p>You can add an introductory text at the beginning of the element to direct the learner's attention to specific topics in the video. Telling the learner that questions will be asked about the topics covered in the video after it ends is an effective way to get people to focus on the video.</p>

<p>It is recommended to keep videos as short as possible. If a video is longer than 1–2 minutes, consider cutting it into two or more parts so that you can add task elements between the videos.</p>

<p>In the <strong>updated version of the Video element</strong>, the learner can be shown the forward button during the video or only after it has ended. In this version, you can define yourself when the forward button is shown to the learner. By default, the forward button is shown automatically when the video ends for videos embedded from supported streaming services such as YouTube and Vimeo, as well as for videos uploaded to Builder. For other videos (e.g. Microsoft Stream or DreamBroker), the forward button is shown in this version by default 10 seconds after the video starts.</p>
