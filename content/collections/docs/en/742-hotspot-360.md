<p>The Hotspot 360° element is very similar to Hotspot 2, but it uses an equirectangular 360° image as the background instead of a two-dimensional image.</p><p>This element can be used when the learner needs to focus on specific topics available in clickable hotspots on top of an equirectangular 360° background image. The clickable hotspots can contain text, images, True/False questions, and/or video files.</p><p>The learner can jump between hotspots by clicking the <strong>Move forward</strong> button on the right side of the window. In <strong>Published</strong> mode, when all mandatory hotspots have been selected, the forward button moves the learner to the next page of the training.</p><h3>Element editing instructions</h3><ul><li><p>Add an equirectangular 360° background image by moving the mouse and selecting "<strong>Change Image</strong>" from the right-side toolbar.</p></li></ul><p><strong>Image requirements:</strong></p><ul><li><p>Equirectangular 360° image</p></li><li><p>Note that 360° images are not automatically optimised, so it is recommended to do this manually before uploading the image: the size should be approximately 6000x3000 – 8000x4000 pixels to achieve good quality at average internet speeds.</p></li><li><p>Drag the clickable hotspot to the correct location on the image.</p></li></ul><p><strong>Note</strong>! You can change the hotspot's colour and icon from the brush icon and adjust its position by clicking the move icon.</p><ul><li><p>Add hotspots by selecting the "<strong>Element Settings…</strong>" option from the gear icon at the top.</p></li><li><p>Click each hotspot to edit its content. A clickable hotspot can contain text, an image, a True/False question, a video file, and a mandatory/not mandatory option.</p></li></ul><ul><li><p>Select the general settings for each hotspot in the element from the tool icons on the left.</p></li></ul><p></p><h3>Element in published mode</h3><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2022/06/hotspot360-2-1.gif"></p><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2022/06/HotSpot-360-1-kuva.png"></p><p><img src="statamic://asset::files::docs/2022/06/HotSpot-360-2-kuva.png"></p>
