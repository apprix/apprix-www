<p>Apprix AI Wizard provides a fast and effortless way to build new online courses.</p>

<p>Once you provide the basic course information and add the necessary materials, the tool automatically assembles them into an interactive and engaging whole.</p>

<p>The goal is to reduce manual work by up to 70–80%, so you can spend your time reviewing content and ensuring quality instead of building a course from scratch.</p>

<p>The link below leads to the Apprix AI Wizard user guide, which will help you use the tool effectively and smoothly.</p>
