Apprix AI Wizard offers a fast and effortless way to build new online courses.

Once you enter the basic course information and upload your materials, the tool automatically transforms them into an interactive and engaging course structure.

The goal is to reduce manual workload by  70–80%, allowing you to focus on reviewing and refining the content rather than creating everything from scratch.

Through the link below, you can access the AI Wizard user guide, which helps you make the most of the tool efficiently and smoothly.

For further information, please see the <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1X9rp_UqU87evcTbvILE9twdoAqUyyhll/view?usp=sharing" target="_blank" rel="noopener">AI Wizard user guide</a>
