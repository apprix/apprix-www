In many organisations, employee onboarding is still handled in a fragmented manner – items to cover are saved in Excel, Word, or shared folders without proper tracking. This creates challenges when onboarding structures are not kept up to date, employees cannot track their own progress, and old onboarding templates are reused without review. With Checklist Engine, various onboarding phases, training modules, task lists, and other items to complete are easily managed, and their progress can be checked at any time.'
