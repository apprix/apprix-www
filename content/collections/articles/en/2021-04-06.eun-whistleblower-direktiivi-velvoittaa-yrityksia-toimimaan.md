        text: 'The purpose of the Whistleblower Directive is to ensure that a person who, in the course of their work, observes or suspects activity contrary to the public interest in specifically defined areas of EU law, can safely report the matter within their own organisation. Organisations are required to implement a secure reporting channel through which employees can report concerns anonymously. In addition, organisations must inform employees about the available reporting options and designate a competent and impartial person or team to monitor and respond to reports.'
