title: 'Why does the content I embedded (e.g. a video or document) not appear to those completing the training?'
answer: '<p>It is possible that the embedded content is not visible because the domain from which the content is embedded is not yet on Apprix''''s list of allowed domains.</p><p>Send Apprix HelpDesk (helpdesk@apprix.fi) the URL of the content being embedded. Alternatively, you can send the entire embed code, as the URL is naturally found within it. Apprix will then add the domain to the list of allowed domains if it is missing. Additionally, the embedded content may not be visible to those completing the training because the settings of the embedded content restrict who can see it. For the content to be visible, you may need to change the visibility settings in the service from which the content is embedded. In the settings it may be necessary to specify who can view the content and to which addresses the content can be embedded. If it relates to Builder, add "builder.apprix.fi" as an allowed address if needed.</p>'
