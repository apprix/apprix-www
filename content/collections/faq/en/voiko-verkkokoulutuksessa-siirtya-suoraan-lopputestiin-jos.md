title: 'Can a learner go directly to the final test in an online training if the training has already been completed previously?'
answer: '<p>No.</p><p>Retaking the course</p><p>Even if a course participant has previously gone through the online training, they must start the course from the beginning for a new completion. This means they must go through all training sections again and complete the same exercises as before.</p><p>Taking the final test</p><p>If the final test is part of the training, the participant takes it at the end of the training. If they do not pass the test, they must take it again. The test can be attempted as many times as necessary until it is passed.</p>'
