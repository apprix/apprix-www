title: '(Pass/Fail element) Why are scores not shown to those who passed the final test?'
answer: '<p>The course uses the Pass/Fail element. With this setting, scores are only shown to course participants who do not pass the final test. Participants who have completed the course have requested that those who pass should also be able to see their scores.</p><p>✅ Solution: Pass/Fail Always</p><p>Pass/Fail Always is an updated version of the element. It allows the user who passed the test to also see their final test score.</p>'
