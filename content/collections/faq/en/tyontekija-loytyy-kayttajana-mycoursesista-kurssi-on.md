title: 'The employee is found as a user in MyCourses, the course is published in Builder and the login settings are MyCourses LMS. They cannot complete the course through MyCourses. What is the problem?'
answer: "<p>When a course is published in Builder and MyCourses LMS is used as its login settings, the user should open the course through MyCourses for login and completion data recording to work correctly.</p><p>If the user has been given a Builder link such as:</p><p>https://builder.apprix.fi/b/e/xxxxx/index.html?mid=...</p><p>...this bypasses MyCourses and results in the course not opening. The correct link always starts with:</p><p>https://mycourses.apprix.fi/index/dashboard/...</p><p>What to do:</p><ul><li>Ensure that the user is given the MyCourses link specifically, not Builder''s direct publish link.</li><li>It may also be good to check that the course has been correctly linked to the MyCourses LMS profile and that the user has the necessary permissions in MyCourses.</li></ul>"
