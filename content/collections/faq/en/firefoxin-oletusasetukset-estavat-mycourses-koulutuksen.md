answer: '<p>Follow the instructions below to allow the browser to open the training in a new tab.</p><ul><li>Select the desired training and click its language button.</li><li>If a new tab does not open, a notification will appear at the top of the page: "Firefox prevented this site from opening a pop-up window."</li><li>Click the notification button and select "Allow pop-ups from apprix.fi"</li><li>Close the new tab that opens, which shows an error message.</li><li>Click the training language button again and the training will open normally.</li></ul>'
