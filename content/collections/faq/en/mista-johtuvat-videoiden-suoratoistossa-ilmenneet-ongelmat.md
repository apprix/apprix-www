title: 'What causes video streaming issues in Builder?'
answer: "<p>If problems occur with video streaming:</p><p>1) Try using a different browser:</p><ul><li>Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome are the most reliable browsers for PC users</li><li>If you are using a Mac, Safari is the best browser option.</li></ul><p>2) Try clearing your entire browser cache.</p><p>Clearing the cache can help with many streaming-related issues as well as other problems.</p><p>3) The issue may be related to hardware acceleration. Check your device''s hardware acceleration settings.</p><p>If the problems relate to Vimeo videos (unable to play videos):</p><p>The following causes may prevent video playback:</p><ul><li>browser extensions</li><li>antivirus software</li><li>firewall</li><li>internet filtering and monitoring software (for example Safe Eyes or Covenant Eyes)</li></ul><p>If you know that you are using any of the above, you can try temporarily disabling it to see if it affects video streaming. Once you know what is preventing video playback, you can try adjusting the settings to allow Vimeo (vimeo.com).</p><p>Other possible causes:</p><p>Using a proxy server can also prevent video playback. If you are using an office network, you can try contacting your network administrator or your organisation''s IT support.</p><p>A final possible cause may be that Transport Layer Security (TLS) is not enabled in your browser. TLS is enabled by default in most browsers. To enable TLS, follow the instructions in your browser''s settings.</p>"
