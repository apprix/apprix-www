title: "When does recording a user's completion in the statistics begin and end?"
answer: '<p>A course completion begins to be recorded as soon as the participant opens the online course (module). For the participant to receive a completion entry, they must go through the course from the beginning to the Finish element. At that point the "Finished" field in the statistics will have the value "1".</p><p>Sometimes it happens that participants do not complete the training all the way to the end and their completion entry is not "finished" but shows as incomplete in the logs and statistics. In this case the "Finished" field will have the value "0". If the user wants to complete the training, they must log in to the training again and complete it up to the Finish element.</p> <img src="/files/docs/2022/04/Tilastot-1-600x141.png" alt="" width="600" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;">'
