title: 'Can a course be put on hold during editing, or can its publication be disabled so that the collected statistics are retained?'
answer: '<p>You can cancel the course publication for the duration of its update. All course completions are retained and continue to appear in the statistics when the course is republished.</p><p>Alternatively, we offer a drafting feature, which allows you to separate a published module from its draft version. With this feature you can work on the draft version of the module without changes being visible in the published — and possibly already in use — version. If this sounds like an interesting solution, more information can be found on our Help page: <a href="/help/89-drafting-ominaisuus">/help/89-drafting-ominaisuus</a></p>'
