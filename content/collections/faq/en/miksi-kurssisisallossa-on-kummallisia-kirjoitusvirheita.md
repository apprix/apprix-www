title: 'Why are there strange typos, characters and symbols in the course content?'
answer: '<p>A course module participant using the Chrome browser may have the Google Translate extension activated. In this case, the Translate extension attempts to translate the page into the language selected by the participant in the extension, for example from Finnish to Finnish or from English to Finnish, even if the original content of the training is in Finnish. This makes the content appear confusing.</p>'
