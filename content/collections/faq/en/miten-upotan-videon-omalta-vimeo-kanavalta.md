title: 'How do I embed a video from my own Vimeo channel?'
answer: '<p>If you have your own Vimeo channel from which you want to embed a video into Builder, you need to set the correct visibility settings for the video.</p><p>Log in to your Vimeo channel.</p><p>If the video is not yet on your channel, select the Upload action, then Choose files, then the video file you want to bring to your Vimeo channel and then embed into Builder.</p><p>Select the video you want to embed into Builder. From the selected video''s page, select Manage privacy (eye icon) and then Hide from Vimeo under the Link heading. This choice limits the video''s visibility so that the video can only be viewed with the embed code and cannot be viewed via the video''s direct link, nor is it publicly visible in the Vimeo service to other users.</p><p>Obtain the video''s embed code. You can find it on the video''s page by selecting Embed and copying the content of the text box or pressing Copy embed code. From the same dialog you can restrict the video''s visibility so that it can only be viewed on Apprix''s sites by selecting Specific domains under the Embed privacy heading and adding the apprix.fi domain to the allowed domains (Allowed domains).</p> <img src="/files/docs/2024/04/vimeo-upotus-1-1.png" alt="" width="602" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;"> <img src="/files/docs/2024/04/vimeo-upotus2-1.png" alt="" width="240" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;"> <img src="/files/docs/2024/04/vimeo-upotus3-1.png" alt="" width="602" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;"> <img src="/files/docs/2024/04/vimeo-upotus-4-1.png" alt="" width="395" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;">'
