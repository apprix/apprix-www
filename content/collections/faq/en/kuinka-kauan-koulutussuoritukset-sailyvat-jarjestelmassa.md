title: 'How long are training completion records retained in the system?'
answer: '<p>The retention of completion records is always decided by the customer as the data controller, who provides Apprix with instructions on how to proceed. Without specific instructions, completion data is retained indefinitely. Apprix can be asked to, for example, automate the anonymisation of completion data (e.g. completion data older than two years is anonymised every 3 months) or to manually delete it. However, these arrangements are always agreed on a customer-specific basis. Customers are recommended to follow the precautionary principle when deleting or anonymising completion data.</p>'
