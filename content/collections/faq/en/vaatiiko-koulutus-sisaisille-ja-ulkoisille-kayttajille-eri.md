title: 'Does a training require different login methods for internal and external users?'
answer: '<p>SSO login (Single Sign-On) is most often intended for internal users. Consumer Login (Personal Passwords) / Email Registration is intended for external users, because through it users can register themselves without a possible internal organisational login. If a course module is intended for both internal and external users, more than one login method may be required.</p>'
