title: 'Why does a streaming service video embedded in a Builder course play in Builder but not through a SCORM package exported to an LMS?'
answer: "<p>The video may not play in the LMS because the settings of the embedded video from the streaming service do not allow the domain of the LMS being used, under which the video content loaded via the SCORM package is embedded. Since the video plays in Builder, the video settings configured in the streaming service likely allow content embedding under the Builder domain (builder.apprix.fi).</p><p>In general, the problem can be solved by allowing video embedding under the required LMS domain in the streaming service''s video settings. For example, in the Vimeo streaming service this is done by navigating to the Vimeo video management page and editing the domains under which the content is allowed to be embedded. More information on changing Vimeo video settings: https://help.vimeo.com/hc/en-us/articles/12426259908881-How-to-embed-my-video</p><p>If a Builder course uses a video embedded from Apprix''s Vimeo channel and this Builder course has not previously been exported as a SCORM package to a customer''s LMS, please send us the LMS domain under which the SCORM package content is displayed, so that we can add the domain to the list of allowed embedding domains in the Vimeo video settings.</p>"
