title: 'When does the forward button appear in the Video element?'
answer: '<p>The Video element settings offer two options:</p><p>1. In the first option, the forward button appears when the video has ended (works only with YouTube, Vimeo and uploaded videos)</p><p>2. In the second option, the forward button appears after a specified amount of time (the time is given in seconds). With this setting the forward button only appears after the specified time has elapsed, regardless of whether the video is fast-forwarded.</p><p>We recommend adding a notice to the video''s opening screen informing viewers that the video must be watched to the end without fast-forwarding.</p><p>Note!</p><p>Video playback is also affected by the internet connection, so if the connection is momentarily slow and the video has not finished loading, it may get stuck waiting for the video to finish loading.</p> <img src="/files/docs/2024/05/VideoSettings-24052024.jpg" alt="" width="548" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;">'
