title: "Why does the training not load in Firefox''s incognito/private window?"
answer: '<p>The reason for the malfunction may be that Firefox''s Enhanced Tracking Protection is enabled by default in incognito/private windows. If the training is opened in a Firefox incognito/private window, Enhanced Tracking Protection must be turned off for the training to load correctly. This is not active in a normal Firefox window, so the setting does not need to be changed there. The setting can be disabled from the menu shown in the image.</p> <img src="/files/docs/2024/08/Firefox-Enhanced-Tracking-Protection.png" alt="" width="761" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;">'
