title: "Can a person with Edit access in User Management also edit their own permissions and everyone else's? In other words, does this setting grant so-called Admin rights?"
answer: "<p>Answer: Yes.</p><p>User Management settings:</p><ul><li>Edit: The user has the right to add new users and assign the necessary permissions to them. This also includes the ability to edit their own and other users'' permissions.</li><li>None: The user does not have the right to add new users or edit permissions.</li></ul>"
