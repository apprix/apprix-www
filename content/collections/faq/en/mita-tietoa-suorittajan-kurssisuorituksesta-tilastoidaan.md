title: "What information about a participant's course completion is recorded in statistics if the module does not have a Login element or other authentication methods?"
answer: "<p>If the module does not have a Login element or other authentication methods and the module is not completed in an LMS system, the completion date, score, progress percentage and information about whether the module was incomplete or completed will be saved in the module''s statistics, but without any identifying information such as a first name or surname. In this case the participant cannot be identified from the module statistics.</p><p>If a separate Login element or other authentication methods are not desired, the only option is to add the course modules to an LMS system. In an LMS system the participant is automatically logged in and the participant''s unique identifier (Student ID) is saved in the statistics so that the participant''s course completion can be linked to the participant.</p>"
