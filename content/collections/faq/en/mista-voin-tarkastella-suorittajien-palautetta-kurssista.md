answer: '<p>You can see feedback left by participants in the Feedback view in the Statistics section. The view shows feedback from participants who have completed the course all the way to the end.</p><p>Written feedback in the Feedback view consists of open feedback that participants may have left in the field on the right side of the Feedback element.</p><p>If you want to view all feedback submitted through the Feedback element, you must open the Big Data view. In that view you can download the completion data you want to review in either CSV or Excel format by clicking Export.</p> <img src="/files/docs/2020/07/stats1-1000x328.png" alt="" width="559" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;"> <img src="/files/docs/2020/07/feedback.png" alt="" width="458" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;"> <img src="/files/docs/2020/07/feedback1-1000x175.png" alt="" width="651" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;">'
