title: 'How is the time spent on a training calculated?'
answer: '<p>The time spent is based on the time when the training page is visible in the browser window. If you switch to another tab in the browser window or hide the training page from the browser window, time calculation stops. Time calculation continues from where it left off when you bring the training page back into view.</p><p>Columns visible in statistics:</p><ul><li>Duration: Time spent on the main module</li><li>Total Duration: Time spent on the main module and submodules, i.e. the total time spent on the entire training</li></ul>'
