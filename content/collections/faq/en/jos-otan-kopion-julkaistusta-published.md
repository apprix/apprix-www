title: 'If I make a copy of a published submodule structure, will the new copy have the main module and submodules in the published state, just like the original module?'
answer: '<p>When you duplicate a published module:</p><ul><li>The new copy of the main module is in Unpublished state</li><li>If the submodules were published in the original submodule structure, they are also directly published in the new copy.</li></ul><p>Note! If submodules are not published (Published state), they will not be visible and selectable in the submodule menu when the module is being completed or previewed.</p><p>In the image above, Submodule 1 is not visible in the Preview mode because it has not been published (there is no green circle before the submodule name in the submodule list).</p> <img src="/files/docs/2020/07/unpublished.png" alt="" width="385" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;"> <img src="/files/docs/2020/07/contents-1000x648.png" alt="" width="414" style="max-width:100%;height:auto;display:block;margin:0.75rem 0;">'
