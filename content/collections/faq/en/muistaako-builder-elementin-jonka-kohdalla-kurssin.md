title: 'Does Builder remember the element where course completion was interrupted, if the course is not completed in one go?'
answer: '<p>If it is a regular module and not a submodule structure, the participant is directed to the element where they interrupted the module completion.</p><p>If it is a submodule structure, the participant is directed to the submodule menu (Submenu buttons element). From the menu the participant can see which submodules they have already completed and continue completing the training. However, partially completed, interrupted modules must be restarted from the beginning of the submodule.</p>'
